What's a stack?

A stack is a pre-assembled combination of Gruntwork Infrastructure Packages that allows you to deploy your entire end-to-end infrastructure in minutes. Each stack include a Network Topology setup, Docker cluster, database, security, continuous integration, automated deployment, and monitoring/alerting, and is configured to work in both dev and your AWS account.
