What is the Gruntwork Subscription?

As a Gruntwork subscriber, you get access to continuous updates, improvements, fixes, and security patches for your Infrastructure Packages, so your infrastructure is always secure and up to date. You also get access to the Gruntwork team of DevOps and infrastructure experts for support, maintenance, questions, and code and design reviews. All for a tiny fraction of the cost of a single full-time DevOps engineer.
