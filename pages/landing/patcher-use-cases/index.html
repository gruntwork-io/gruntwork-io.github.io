Patcher will scan your codebase and open a pull request, such as the one shown in the screenshot below, that transforms your code to use the new version of Terraform. For example, Terraform 0.12 had a particularly painful backwards incompatible upgrade, but Patcher can make such upgrades as painless and smooth as reviewing and merging a PR. At Gruntwork, we have created patches to automatically do each of the incremental upgrades.
